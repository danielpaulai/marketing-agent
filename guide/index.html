TROUBLE?

Quick fixes

I cannot find Plugins in the app

Look under Settings, then Customize, then Plugins. If you do not see it, you are likely on the free plan (Claude Code needs Pro or Max) or the app needs an update. In a terminal session of Claude Code, typing /plugin opens the same manager.

The marketplace will not add

Check the URL is exactly https://github.com/danielpaulai/marketing-agent and that you are online, then update the app and retry.

I installed but nothing changed

Plugins load when a session starts. Start a new session (or restart the app) and the skills are live.

FLOW 1

Get measured: extract your Voice DNA

Start a new session in Claude Code and choose a folder for your agent to live in. Make a new one called my-marketing-agent. This folder is your agent's home, everything it learns lives here.

Gather your raw material first (10 minutes of copy-paste): 5 or more posts you wrote yourself, one voice note or call transcript (90+ seconds of you talking), and a few DM or email replies to real clients. The messier and more human, the better.

Then paste this:

Extract my voice. Here is my raw material: [paste your 5+ posts, your transcript, and a few client replies]. My brand accent color is [hex or "pick one for me"], my name is [name], and my one-line tagline is [tagline].

FLOW 2

Write your first post, in detail

Same folder, same session or a new one. Paste:

Write me a post about [your topic, plus any raw material: a story, a result, a belief, a lesson].

What happens, beat by beat:

  • The 5 hooks come first. Five different psychological angles (Result Lead, Confession, Contrarian, Curiosity Gap, Pattern Interrupt), each under 12 words. Pick a number, or say "use the strongest one".
  • The post gets written in YOUR voice, hook unchanged, a rehook line, short paragraphs, one CTA, and a P.S. It reads your VOICE-DNA.md before writing a word.
  • It scores itself on the Invisibility Diagnostic (Voice, POV, Specificity, Asking). Below 4/4, it fixes and rescores before you ever see it.
  • You get 3 pinned comment options and an animated LinkedIn-style preview card of your post.

FLOW 3

Connect Apify: give your agent research arms

Your skills work with zero connectors. But connected to Apify, your agent can research what is trending in your niche TODAY and write from live data instead of memory. This is the upgrade you watched on stage. Do it at home, it takes 10 minutes.

Set it up (once)

  • 1. Create a free account at apify.com (the free plan includes monthly credits, no card needed).
  • 2. In Claude, open Settings › Connectors › Add custom connector and paste: https://mcp.apify.com
  • 3. A browser window opens, log in to Apify and approve. That is the whole connection.
  • 4. Test it: in a new session say "Using Apify, find 3 things trending in [your niche] this week." If results come back, you are wired.

The Master Prompt (copy, paste, run)

This is the full daily engine in one prompt: live research, one chosen idea, a scored post in your voice, the image prompt, all saved to a file. Run it any morning, or hand it to your routine below.

You are my Marketing Agent, my daily content engine. Run the full production cycle now, start to finish, and do not stop to ask me questions unless a rule below forces you to.

MY CONTEXT, READ FIRST
Read VOICE-DNA.md in this folder, top to bottom, before anything else. It is the only source of truth for my voice (markers, rhythm, vocabulary, banned words, signature phrases, format habits), my brand (section 8), and my business (section 9: what I sell, who I sell to, the result I deliver, my content pillars). Every sentence you produce must obey it. Never invent facts, results, numbers, client names, or stories about me. If a specific is missing and needed, use a clearly marked placeholder like [X%] and flag it at the end.

STEP 1, RESEARCH (Apify)
Using the Apify connector, research what is moving for my audience right now:
a) What my ideal customers (section 9) are discussing this week: pains, debates, questions, news.
b) What is trending inside my content pillars in the last 7 days.
Bring back exactly 3 candidate ideas. For each: the insight in one line, why it matters right now, where you found it, and which pillar it serves.
If Apify is not connected or a search fails, do not stop. Fall back to your own knowledge of my pillars and label each idea [NOT LIVE RESEARCH].

STEP 2, CHOOSE
Pick the strongest of the 3 using: relevance to my ideal customer, freshness, and my right to win the topic (my authority). State your pick and your reason in one line.

STEP 3, WRITE (use the write-post skill standards)
First write 5 hooks, each a different type: Result Lead, Confession, Contrarian Challenge, Curiosity Gap, Pattern Interrupt. Each under 12 words. Select the strongest yourself and say why in one sentence.
Then write the full post in MY voice: the chosen hook unchanged, a rehook line that deepens the tension, body paragraphs of 3 lines maximum, exactly one CTA, and a P.S. as the most human line. Length 1,300 to 1,500 characters. Kill every pattern on the AI blacklist. Never use an em dash or en dash anywhere.

STEP 4, SCORE
Score the post on the Invisibility Diagnostic: Voice, POV, Specificity, Asking. If it is below 4/4, fix the failing dimension and rescore before continuing. Show the final score.

STEP 5, THE VISUAL
Write one complete, self-contained image-generation prompt for this post using my brand colors and platform size, ready to paste into any image tool.

STEP 6, DELIVER
Save everything into today-post.md in this folder: today's date, the idea and its source, the final post, the diagnostic score, 3 pinned comment options (one bonus insight, one audience question, one soft plug), and the image prompt. Then show me the post text in a copyable block and finish with one line: the idea you would run tomorrow.

Printed length is about 500 words. It chains research, choice, writing, scoring, visual, and delivery into one run. Change nothing on first use, tune it only after you have run it three times.

FLOW 4

The routine: your standing order (runs locally)

This is Level 3. The routine runs on your computer, so it needs the conditions below to be true, check all five:

  • Your computer is on and awake at the scheduled time (plugged in, lid open or sleep disabled for that hour).
  • Claude Code can run, you are signed in and the app opens without asking you anything.
  • Always the same folder. The routine must run inside my-marketing-agent, where VOICE-DNA.md lives.
  • VOICE-DNA.md exists and is current. The agent cannot sound like you while you sleep without the ledger.
  • Say the timezone. "7am" means your local 7am, name it when you set the routine.

Set it (paste in your agent folder)

Create a routine that runs locally on this computer every morning at 7am my time (timezone: [your timezone]) inside this folder. Each run: read VOICE-DNA.md, then execute the Master Prompt saved in master-prompt.md from research through delivery, and leave the finished post, score, pinned comments, and image prompt in today-post.md. If anything fails, write what happened into today-post.md instead of failing silently. Confirm the schedule back to me.

Save the Master Prompt from Flow 3 as master-prompt.md in your agent folder first, then the routine above can call it every morning. Ask your agent: "save the master prompt I just gave you as master-prompt.md".

Not ready to schedule? The manual morning

Same power, one action: open Claude Code in your folder each morning with coffee and say "run my master prompt". Do that for three days. When you trust the output, set the routine and stop showing up.
